# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 29, 2026**

## Introduction

AffirmUp ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Preferences**: Theme settings, notification preferences, and favorite affirmations stored locally on your device.
- **Custom Affirmations**: Any custom affirmations you create are stored locally on your device.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data**: We may collect anonymous usage statistics such as app opens, features used, and session duration to improve our service.
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and troubleshooting.
- **Advertising Data**: We use Google AdMob to display ads in the free version. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the app's functionality
- To improve and optimize user experience
- To display relevant advertisements (free version only)
- To process in-app purchases
- To respond to customer support requests

## Data Storage

All personal data, including favorites, custom affirmations, streak history, and preferences, is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. We do not transmit this data to external servers.

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:
- **Google AdMob**: For displaying advertisements (free version). [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Google Play / Apple App Store**: For processing in-app purchases and app distribution.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data (stored locally on your device)
- Delete your data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app
- Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
